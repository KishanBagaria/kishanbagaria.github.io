Adding Yourself

There is only one rule you must follow if you want to listed here, and that is you must return all llamas you get and return them in a month or you'll be removed.

Also, it's no rule but if you install One Click Llama Button, it'll be very easy to return llamas. I'm surprised to find people who haven't got OCLB installed asking me to add them to their list. I could turn this list private, viewable to only those who have OCLB installed.

If those things are good with you, just send me a note saying "add me in the OCLB Exchangers List?"

  • You'll be added only if your llama give ratio (given / received) is more than 0.9. I'll tell you if it is not and you can increase this by returning the llamas you got.
  • You'll be removed from the list if:
    • You don't return any llamas for 4 weeks straight (because you're taking a hiatus or llamas from another planet abducted you). You can ask me to add you again after you return all llamas.
    • You deactivate your account.
    • You receive 100,000 llamas.
