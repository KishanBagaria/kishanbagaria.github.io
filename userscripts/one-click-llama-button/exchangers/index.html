Adding Yourself

If you give a llama to anyone who gives you one, you can are eligible to be added on this list. Just send me a note with subject as "Add me in OCLB Exchangers List".

  • You'll only be added if your llama give ratio (given / received) is more than 0.9. I'll tell you if it is not and you can increase this by giving more llamas.
  • If you've been added on the list, you'll be removed from it if:
    • You don't return any llamas for 4 weeks straight (because you're taking a hiatus or aliens abducted you). You can ask me to add you again after you return all llamas.
    • You receive 100,000 llamas.
